But the memory of a spider first came from Marquez: while still practically a child she had sneaked out of her parents’ house to go to a dance, and while she was coming back through the woods after having danced all night without permission, a fearful thunderclap split the sky in two and through the crack came the lightning bolt of brimstone that changed her into a spider.
